The problem

Manual behavior logging is inconsistent, time-consuming, and often restricted from using cameras in school settings.

Our solution

BehavioTrack uses wearable + beacon sensors to pre-label likely events, then lets you confirm with one tap—fast, private, and accurate.

Key features

AI-assisted logging

Sensor fusion models map signals to your operational definitions—no cameras required.

Auto session notes

One-tap 4-for-4 and ABC summaries with progress graphs and exportable PDFs.

Offline & secure

Works without internet; end-to-end encryption with role-based access.

Caregiver sharing

Simple, permissioned updates for parents, teachers, and supervisors.
